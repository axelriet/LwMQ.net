About LwMQ

LwMQ has been imagined, designed and implemented by Axel Rietschin in response to the need for a lightweight, efficient messaging solution covering local and remote inter-process communication over the fastest protocols and transports available for low latency and high throughput through a DMA-first architecture.

Axel is a multi-time founder and author of widely used software applications and components. He is also a former Windows Kernel engineer from the Windows Core OS team at Microsoft, having contributed to the NT Kernel as well as the Windows Container virtualization platform.

LwMQ is a product of Axel Rietschin Software development, LLC, a Washington State limited liability company founded by Axel Rietschin in 2023.

Axel Rietschin Software Development, LLC is a Microsoft Partner member of the Microsoft Windows Hardware Compatibility Program (WHCP) and is committed to ensuring LwMQ’s compatibility with Windows and its ecosystem, as well as to providing the best possible experience for our users.
